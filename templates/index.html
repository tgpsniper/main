POLICIES ON ADMISSION

Since this is a non-sectarian school, no discrimination is made on the ground of religion in the admission of pupils/students to Jiane Therese International School. Admission is granted with the definite understanding that the pupil/student and his/her parents or guardians agree with the standards and requirements of the school. A. Classifications of Students Pupils/Students are classified with reference to their year of entry, age, and the year level that they have finished. Nursery/ Beginner - Pupils whose ages range from 3 to 5 years old by June, who is never gone to school. Kinder 1 - Pupils whose ages range from 4 to 5 years old by June and who finished Nursery/Day Care. Preparatory - Age qualification for kindergarten learners in public schools should be five (5) years old by June 1 of every school year (DepEd Order No. 16, s. 2015). However, the school may consider learners entering Kindergarten who will turn five (5) years old by the end of August on the condition that the Philippine Early Childhood Development (ECD) Checklist must be administered to the learner to ensure that the learner can meet the expectations of the grade level. (Deped Order No. 47 s. 2016) Grade 1- Children who have completed DepEd-accredited Kindergarten programs are eligible for Grade 1. Level 7- Pupils who have finished their elementary course and eligible for promotion to high school. Old Students: Students who were enrolled and have finished the previous school year in Jiane Therese International School. Transferee: Students who were enrolled in other institutions in the previous school year
